Rules for Participants

  • Access to Data: The EmoArt dataset is available under a CC BY-NC 4.0 license for academic research.
  • Use of Pre-trained Models: Participants may freely use pre-trained weights obtained from open-source models (e.g., FLUX.1, SDXL).
  • Evaluation Integrity: Official rankings are computed on a hidden test set evaluated securely to prevent data leakage.
  • Submission Format: Participants will submit generated images (Track 1) or prediction files/code (Track 2). Docker containers may be required for verification.
  • Evaluation Metrics:
    • Track 1: FID, Attribute Alignment Score (AAS), and LPIPS.
    • Track 2: Top-1 Accuracy and Macro F1-score for Emotion, Valence, and Arousal.
  • Human Evaluation: Top-ranked submissions will undergo qualitative assessment by art and design experts.
  • Ethical Guidelines: Generation of deepfakes or offensive content is strictly prohibited.
